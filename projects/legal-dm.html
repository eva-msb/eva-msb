LEGAL-DECISION MAKING & AI

AI Governance // Empathy // Legal Discretion // Human-AI Interaction

AI is increasingly used in legal decision-making, with systems such as COMPAS being implemented under the promise of enhancing fairness, precision, and efficiency. Yet such systems also reconfigure how human discretion and responsibility are exercised. This project examines how AI recommendations influence both decision outcomes and the affective processes that underpin discretionary reasoning. It tests whether algorithmic advice changes affective engagement and redistributes responsibility in legal decision-making, offering empirical insight into the challenges of human oversight and ethical governance under the EU AI Act.
