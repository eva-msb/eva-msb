FACIAL AI FILTERS

AI-mediation // Empathy // Prosocial Decision-Making // Affect Modulation

Facial expressions play a central role in social interactions, conveying key emotional information that shapes how we perceive and respond to others. AI technologies now enable real-time manipulation of facial expressions in interaction partners, raising ethical questions about AI-mediated social perception. Across three experiments, we investigated whether AI filters that alter emotional facial expressions of others alter empathy and prosocial behavior, and whether such filters are used strategically to regulate one's own affective engagement. Participants viewed donation appeals in authentic or AI-altered form and made incentivized donation decisions.
